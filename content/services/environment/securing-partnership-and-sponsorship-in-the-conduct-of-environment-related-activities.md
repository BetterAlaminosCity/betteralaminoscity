lastUpdated: "2026-07-24"
office: "City Environment and Natural Resources Office"
classification: "Simple"

requirements:
  - item: "Letter Intent"
    whereToSecure: "The letter request must be prepared by the client."
  - item: "Memorandum of Agreement (for at least 6 months partnership)"
    whereToSecure: "Proposed Memorandum of Agreement must be submitted by the proponent or interested party."
steps:
  - clientStep: "Inquire about the possible arrangements, requirements on logistics and schedule"
    agencyAction: "Answer the inquiries and provide additional necessary information."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III, Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Submit a Letter of Intent addressed to the City Mayor to the City Archives Office"
    agencyAction: "2.1. Act based on the issued Action Slip by the City Administrator or instruction by the City Mayor"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III, Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Submit a Letter of Intent addressed to the City Mayor to the City Archives Office"
    agencyAction: "2.2. Confirmation of schedule and agree on the necessary arrangements."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III, Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide"
  - clientStep: "Submit a Letter of Intent addressed to the City Mayor to the City Archives Office"
    agencyAction: "2.3. Request a resolution from the Sangguniang Panlungsod for City Mayor's authority to sign to a Memorandum of Agreement or send a confirmation letter/form to the interested party whichever is applicable."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 working days"
    personResponsible: "CGDH I-CENRO or Engineer III"
  - clientStep: "Memorandum of Agreement signing"
    agencyAction: "Facilitate signing of the Memorandum of Agreement upon the issuance of resolution of Authority to Sign for the City Mayor from the Sangguniang Panlungsod"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "30 minutes"
    personResponsible: "CGDH I-CENRO, Engineer III and Environmental Management Specialist II"
  - clientStep: "Conduct/Participate in the activity."
    agencyAction: "Require participants to fill-up the attendance forms"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "2 to 4 hours depending on the nature of activity"
    personResponsible: "Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Conduct/Participate in the activity."
    agencyAction: "Document the activity"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "Not specified in the Citizen's Charter"
    personResponsible: "Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Conduct/Participate in the activity."
    agencyAction: "Provide certificates of partnership and sponsorship"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "1 minute"
    personResponsible: "Environmental Management Specialist II or Administrative Aide I"
totalProcessingTime: "10 days, 5 hours and 1 minute"
totalFees: "None"

To forge partnerships in the conduct of environmental activities such as but not limited to mangrove or tree propagation and planting, SCUBAsurero, clean-up drives and financial or logistical assistance.
