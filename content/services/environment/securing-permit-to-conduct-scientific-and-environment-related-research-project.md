---
title: "Securing Permit to conduct Scientific and Environment Related Research Project"
description: "To issue permits in the conduct of scientific and environment related research project in order ensure compliance of the researchers to the environment friendly and ethical procedures in the conduct of research in the City of Alaminos."
lastUpdated: "2026-07-24"
office: "City Environment and Natural Resources Office"
classification: "Simple"
transactionType: "G2C - Government to Citizens, G2B - Government to Business, G2G - Government to Government"
whoMayAvail: "Individual, Group or Research Institutions conducting research involving sampling and collection of specimens, surveys and monitoring of environmental parameters."
requirements:
  - item: "Letter of Intent or request letter duly endorsed by the school/university or institution."
    whereToSecure: "The letter request must be prepared by the client."
  - item: "Copy of research proposal."
    whereToSecure: "A research proposal prepared by the researcher/s."
  - item: "Filled up request for permit to conduct research form."
    whereToSecure: "Form provided by the City Environment and Natural Resources Office."
  - item: "Notarized Undertaking."
    whereToSecure: "Format provided by the City Environment and Natural Resources Office."
  - item: "Prior Informed Consent (PIC) when applicable."
    whereToSecure: "PIC from other agencies. PIC Issued by the concern National Agencie/s"
steps:
  - clientStep: "Inquire about the possible arrangements, requirements on the logistics and schedule"
    agencyAction: "1.1 Answer the inquiries and provide additional necessary information."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Submit a Letter of Intent or request letter and other requirements to the City Archives Office or through email to the city's official accounts."
    agencyAction: "2.1 Act based on the issued Action Slip by the City Administrator or instruction by the City Mayor. 2.2 Provide copy of the request for permit to conduct research form."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "5 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Secure a Prior Informed Consent and Permit"
    agencyAction: "2.1. Review of the completeness of the requirements. 2.2. Issuance of a Prior Informed Consent Form signed by the City Mayor."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "5 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Administrative Aide I and CGDHI -City Environment and Natural Resources Office"
  - clientStep: "Conduct of the research activity"
    agencyAction: "3.1. Assist during the conduct of the research study. 3.2. Provide the requested references and data."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "(cannot be assumed)"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III, Environmental Specialist II, Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Feedback Results"
    agencyAction: "Facilitate a conference for the feedbacking of results."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "30 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Engineer III or Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Provide the LGU a copy of the results or final paper. (published or unpublished)"
    agencyAction: "Accept a copy of the research paper."
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "5 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Administrative Aide I"
totalProcessingTime: "55 minutes"
totalFees: "None"
---

To issue permits in the conduct of scientific and environment related research project in order ensure compliance of the researchers to the environment friendly and ethical procedures in the conduct of research in the City of Alaminos.
