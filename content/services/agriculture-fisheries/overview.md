---
title: "Overview"
description: "What the Agriculture & Fisheries office offers to local farmers and fisherfolk."
---

The City Agriculture Office supports farmers and fisherfolk through extension services, seedling and input distribution, and registration for national agricultural assistance programs. Fisherfolk operating within Alaminos City waters, including areas near the Hundred Islands, can register for local fisherfolk identification and access seasonal advisories. Contact your barangay agricultural technician for the latest program schedules.
