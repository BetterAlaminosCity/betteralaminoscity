---
title: "Overview"
description: "Using city libraries, sports facilities, and government properties, and permits for public events."
---

The City Library Office provides public access to library materials, computers, and reference services, while the City Youth and Sports Development Office manages reservations for the Alaminos City Sports Complex. The City General Services Office processes requests to borrow government properties, and the City Archives Office issues the Mayor's Clearance and special permits for parades, fund-raising activities, and similar public events.
