---
title: "Overview"
description: "Health services available through barangay health centers and the City Health Office."
---

Every barangay in Alaminos City has a health center offering basic consultations, maternal and child health services, and immunizations at no cost. The City Health Office coordinates city-wide health programs, including dengue prevention campaigns and seasonal vaccination drives. Residents needing specialized care are referred to Alaminos City-run or partner hospitals.
