---
title: "Overview"
description: "Social welfare assistance programs administered by the City Social Welfare and Development Office."
---

The City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO) administers assistance programs for indigent families, senior citizens, and persons with disabilities, including financial aid, senior citizen ID issuance, and referrals to national programs such as the Department of Social Welfare and Development's assistance-to-individuals-in-crisis program. Walk-in applicants are assessed on a case-by-case basis.
