---
title: "Overview"
description: "How Alaminos City prepares residents for typhoons, flooding, and other hazards."
---

The City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office (CDRRMO) coordinates evacuation planning, early warning advisories, and relief operations during typhoons and other hazards common to coastal Pangasinan. Residents in low-lying and coastal barangays are encouraged to know their nearest evacuation center in advance. Emergency hotlines are published through official city channels ahead of each typhoon season.
