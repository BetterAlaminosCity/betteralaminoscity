---
title: "Overview"
description: "Collection schedules and segregation requirements for household and business waste."
---

Garbage collection schedules vary by barangay and are typically posted at barangay halls and along collection routes. Residents are required to segregate biodegradable, non-biodegradable, and recyclable waste under the city's solid waste management ordinance. Bulky waste and construction debris generally require a separate arrangement with the City Environment and Natural Resources Office.
