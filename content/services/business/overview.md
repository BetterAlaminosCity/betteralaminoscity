---
title: "Overview"
description: "How to apply for or renew a business permit in Alaminos City."
---

New businesses operating within Alaminos City must secure a Mayor's Permit alongside their business registration with the appropriate national agency (DTI for sole proprietorships, SEC for corporations and partnerships). Annual renewal is typically due in January. The Business Permits and Licensing Office can advise on required clearances, including barangay clearance, fire safety inspection, and sanitary permit.
