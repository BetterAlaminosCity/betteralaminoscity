    whereToSecure: "Management Information System (MIS)"
  - item: "Kontrata ng Upa (photocopy, kung na-expire na) / Sertipiko mula sa Alaminos City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office (photocopy) / Inspection and Recommendation slip mula sa City Market & Cemetery Section Office"
    whereToSecure: "May-ari ng Ari-arian / City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office / City Market & Cemetery Section Office"
  - item: "Lisensya mula sa mga Pambansang Ahensya ng Pamahalaan (photocopy) - kung may pagbabagong gagawin"
    whereToSecure: "BSP, FDA, DENR, DFA, PCSO, DOLE, DOE, DOT, HLURB/DHSUD, at iba pang kinauukulang ahensya (kung kinakailangan)"
  - item: "Certificate of Registration para sa Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, o Corporation (photocopy) - kung may pagbabagong gagawin"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"

    agencyAction: "Beripikahin ng BPLO-1 na kumpleto ang lahat ng kinakailangang pre-requirement"
    fee: "Wala"
    processingTime: "10 minuto"
    personResponsible: "License Inspector I, Licensing Officer II"

    processingTime: "30 minuto"
    personResponsible: "City Treasurer's Office (CTO) Staff"
  - clientStep: "Kunin ang Mayor's Permit at sticker"
    agencyAction: "I-print at ipalabas ng BPLO-2 ang Mayor's Permit at sticker"
    fee: "Wala"
    processingTime: "20 minuto"
