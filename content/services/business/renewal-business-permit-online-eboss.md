title: "Online Filing of Application for Renewal of Business Permit (eBOSS)"
description: "Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for businesses renewing online through the eBOSS/eLGU system."
lastUpdated: "2026-07-22"
office: "City Business Permit and Licensing Office"
classification: "Simple"
transactionType: "G2B"
whoMayAvail: "All"
requirements:
  - item: "Real Property Tax Clearance"
    whereToSecure: "City Treasurer's Office"
  - item: "Barangay Clearance (if not yet integrated)"
    whereToSecure: "Barangay Hall (Amadiego, Cayucay, Linmansangan, Mona, Quibuar, Sabangan, San Antonio, Victoria)"
  - item: "If leasee: photocopy of Contract of Lease"
    whereToSecure: "Lessor"
  - item: "Certification for tourism-related business (hotel, transient house, lodging house, resort and cottages, and businesses within the HINP vicinity)"
    whereToSecure: "Alaminos City Tourism and Cultural Affairs Office"
  - item: "Certification for rolling stores"
    whereToSecure: "City Market and Cemetery Section"
  - item: "Special Power of Attorney (SPA) or Authorization Letter from the business owner"
    whereToSecure: "Notary Public, Business Owner"
  - item: "License from National Government Agencies - if there are changes to be made"
    whereToSecure: "BSP, FDA, DENR, DFA, PCSO, DOLE, DOE, DOT, HLURB/DHSUD, PNP, and other concerned agencies (if needed)"
  - item: "Certificate of Registration for Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, or Corporation - if there are changes to be made"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"
  - item: "Proof of Gross Receipts (audited financial statement, sworn declaration of gross sales or receipts, or income tax returns)"
    whereToSecure: "Owner"
steps:
  - clientStep: 'Log in to the eLGU City of Alaminos Pangasinan portal or the eGOV mobile app, select "City of Alaminos Services" then "Business Permit (BPLS)" then "Renewal" then "Proceed," fill in the needed information, upload documents, and submit'
    agencyAction: "Verify the submitted pre-requirements"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "15 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Licensing Officer II, License Inspector I, Administrative Assistant III"
  - clientStep: "Pay the assessed fees online through QR-Ph Code"
    agencyAction: "Verify the assessment of gross sales/receipts, then approve the assessment and payment"
    fee: "Account Clearance Php 50.00; Building Clearance Php 20.00; Sanitary Permit Php 100.00; Sanitary Inspection (based on area); Fire Safety Inspection Certificate: 15% of total fees and charges; Real Property Tax Clearance Php 50.00; ICT Fee Php 25.00; Documentary Stamp Php 90.00"
    processingTime: "1 hour and 50 minutes"
    personResponsible: "City Treasurer's Office Staff"
  - clientStep: "Claim your permit: visit the Business One-Stop-Shop (BOSS) to pick up your Mayor's Permit, then secure post-requirements within 30 working days (Sanitary Permit, Building Clearance, BFP-FSIC) to remain compliant; failure to comply is grounds for temporary closure of the business"
    agencyAction: "Approve and print the Mayor's Permit"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Licensing Officer II, Licensing Officer I"
totalProcessingTime: "2 hours and 15 minutes"
totalFees: "Php 335.00 + Mayor's Permit Fee (depends on gross sales/receipts) + 15% of total fees and charges (FSIC) + Sanitary Inspection Fee (based on area)"
---

Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for businesses renewing online through the eBOSS/eLGU system.
