---
title: "On-site Application for New Business Permit"
description: "Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for new businesses applying on-site."
lastUpdated: "2026-07-22"
office: "City Business Permit and Licensing Office"
classification: "Simple"
transactionType: "G2B"
whoMayAvail: "All"
requirements:
  - item: "Community Tax Certificate (CTC), original"
    whereToSecure: "Barangay Hall or City Treasurer's Office"
  - item: "Barangay Clearance (1 photocopy) - if not yet integrated in iBPLS"
    whereToSecure: "Where the business establishment is located"
  - item: "Owner and Representative enrollment in the Personal Information System (PIS)"
    whereToSecure: "Management Information System (MIS)"
  - item: "Contract of Lease (if lessee) / Mayor's Permit of the Lessor / Letter of Consent / Tax Declaration of the Property (photocopy) / Certification from Alaminos City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office (photocopy) / Inspection and Recommendation slip from City Market & Cemetery Section Office"
    whereToSecure: "Property Owner / Alaminos City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office / City Market & Cemetery Office"
  - item: "Special Power of Attorney (SPA) or Authorization Letter from the business owner (photocopy)"
    whereToSecure: "Business Owner"
  - item: "License from National Government Agencies (photocopy, if applicable)"
    whereToSecure: "BSP, FDA, DENR, DFA, PCSO, DOLE, DOE, DOT, HLURB/DHSUD, and other concerned agencies (if needed)"
  - item: "Certificate of Registration for Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, or Corporation (photocopy)"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"
steps:
  - clientStep: "Proceed to the Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS) for submission of pre-requirements and the application form for the Mayor's Permit"
    agencyAction: "BPLO-1 verifies that all pre-requirements are secured and the application form is properly filled out, encodes the application, and issues a one-time assessment for the Mayor's Permit and other regulatory fees"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "25 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Licensing Officer II, License Inspector I, Administrative Aide IV, Administrative Aide I"
  - clientStep: "Pay the Mayor's Permit and other regulatory fees"
    agencyAction: "City Treasurer's Office (CTO) accepts payment of the Mayor's Permit and other regulatory fees"
    fee: "Mayor's Permit Fee (depends on capitalization, see Revenue Code Chapter III, Permit and Regulatory Fees Article A); Business Plate Php 200.00; Business Inspection Fee (depends on type/nature of business); Zoning Clearance Php 20.00; Real Property Tax Clearance Php 50.00; Sanitary Permit Php 100.00; Sanitary Inspection (based on area); Documentary Stamp Php 90.00; ICT Fee Php 25.00"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "City Treasurer's Office (CTO) Staff"
  - clientStep: "Secure the Mayor's Permit"
    agencyAction: "BPLO-2 prints and releases the Mayor's Permit and Business Plate"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "25 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Administrative Aide III, Licensing Officer I, Licensing Officer II"
totalProcessingTime: "60 minutes (Zoning Clearance, Real Property Tax Clearance, Sanitary Permit, and Fire Safety Inspection Certificate must be complied with within 30 calendar days from release of the Mayor's/Business Permit)"
totalFees: "Php 485.00 + Mayor's Permit Fee (depends on capitalization and nature of business) + 15% of total fees and charges (FSIC) + Sanitary Inspection Fee (based on area)"
---

Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for new businesses applying on-site.
