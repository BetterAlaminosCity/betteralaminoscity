---
title: "On-site Application for Renewal of Business Permit"
description: "Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for businesses renewing on-site."
lastUpdated: "2026-07-22"
office: "City Business Permit and Licensing Office"
classification: "Simple"
transactionType: "G2B"
whoMayAvail: "All"
requirements:
  - item: "Real Property Tax Clearance"
    whereToSecure: "City Treasurer's Office"
  - item: "Barangay Clearance (1 photocopy) - if not yet integrated in iBPLS"
    whereToSecure: "Where the business establishment is located"
  - item: "Owner and Representative enrollment in the Personal Information System (PIS)"
    whereToSecure: "Management Information System (MIS)"
  - item: "Contract of Lease (photocopy, if expired) / Certification from Alaminos City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office (photocopy) / Inspection and Recommendation slip from City Market & Cemetery Section Office"
    whereToSecure: "Property Owner / City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office / City Market & Cemetery Section Office"
  - item: "License from National Government Agencies (photocopy) - if there are changes to be made"
    whereToSecure: "BSP, FDA, DENR, DFA, PCSO, DOLE, DOE, DOT, HLURB/DHSUD, and other concerned agencies (if needed)"
  - item: "Certificate of Registration for Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, or Corporation (photocopy) - if there are changes to be made"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"
steps:
  - clientStep: "Proceed to the Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS) and submit the required pre-requirements"
    agencyAction: "BPLO-1 verifies that all required pre-requirements are complete"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "License Inspector I, Licensing Officer II"
  - clientStep: "Assessment and payment"
    agencyAction: "City Treasurer's Office (CTO) assesses gross sales/receipts and accepts payment of business taxes and other fees"
    fee: "Account Clearance Php 50.00; Building Clearance Php 20.00; Sanitary Permit Php 100.00; Sanitary Inspection (based on area); Documentary Stamp Php 60.00; ICT Fee Php 25.00"
    processingTime: "30 minutes"
    personResponsible: "City Treasurer's Office (CTO) Staff"
  - clientStep: "Secure the Mayor's Permit and sticker"
    agencyAction: "BPLO-2 prints and releases the Mayor's Permit and sticker"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "20 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Administrative Aide I, Administrative Aide III, Administrative Aide IV, Licensing Officer I, Licensing Officer II"
totalProcessingTime: "60 minutes (Account Clearance, Building Clearance, Sanitary Permit, and Fire Safety Inspection Certificate must be complied with within 30 calendar days from release of the Mayor's/Business Permit)"
totalFees: "Php 225.00 + Mayor's Permit Fee and Business Tax (depends on gross sales/receipts) + 15% of total fees and charges (FSIC) + Sanitary Inspection Fee (based on area)"
---

Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for businesses renewing on-site.
