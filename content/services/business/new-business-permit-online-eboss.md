---
title: "Online Filing of Application for New Business Permit (eBOSS)"
description: "Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for new businesses applying online through the eBOSS/eLGU system."
lastUpdated: "2026-07-22"
office: "City Business Permit and Licensing Office"
classification: "Simple"
transactionType: "G2B"
whoMayAvail: "All"
requirements:
  - item: "Community Tax Certificate (CTC), current year"
    whereToSecure: "Barangay Hall or City Treasurer's Office"
  - item: "Barangay Clearance (if not yet integrated)"
    whereToSecure: "Barangay Hall (Amadiego, Cayucay, Linmansangan, Mona, Quibuar, Sabangan, San Antonio, Victoria)"
  - item: "If owned: photocopy of Tax Declaration"
    whereToSecure: "Lessor"
  - item: "If leasee: photocopy of Contract of Lease and Mayor's Permit of the Lessor"
    whereToSecure: "Property Owner"
  - item: "If free of use: Letter of Consent from the Property Owner and Tax Declaration of the property (building/lot)"
    whereToSecure: "Property Owner"
  - item: "Certification for tourism-related business (hotel, transient house, lodging house, resort and cottages, and businesses within the HINP vicinity)"
    whereToSecure: "Alaminos City Tourism and Cultural Affairs Office"
  - item: "Certification for rolling stores"
    whereToSecure: "City Market and Cemetery Section"
  - item: "For single proprietorship/OPC/Partnership: Special Power of Attorney (SPA) or Authorization Letter from the business owner"
    whereToSecure: "Notary Public, Business Owner, Corporate Secretary"
  - item: "For Corporation: Secretary's Certificate (if the permit is processed by a representative)"
    whereToSecure: "Notary Public, Business Owner, Corporate Secretary"
  - item: "License from National Government Agencies (if applicable)"
    whereToSecure: "BSP, FDA, DENR, DFA, PCSO, CDA, DOLE, DOE, DOT, HLURB/DHSUD, PNP, and other concerned agencies (if needed)"
  - item: "For single proprietorship: DTI Business Name registration"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"
  - item: "For Corporation/Partnership/OPC: SEC registration with Articles of Incorporation"
    whereToSecure: "DTI, SEC"
steps:
  - clientStep: 'Log in to the eLGU City of Alaminos Pangasinan portal (elgu-city-of-alaminos-pangasinan.e.gov.ph) or the eGOV mobile app, select "City of Alaminos Services" then "Business Permit (BPLS)" then "New" then "Proceed," fill in business info/operations/line of business, upload documents, and submit the application'
    agencyAction: "View the application and print the transmittal; view the account, check requirements for completeness, start assessment, assess the application, update status, verify fees, add remarks, generate the BIN; if incomplete, reject the application; endorse to concerned offices (City Health Office, City Engineering Office - Zoning, BFP)"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "15 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Licensing Officer II, Licensing Officer I, Administrative Assistant III"
  - clientStep: "Pay the assessed fees online through QR-Ph Code"
    agencyAction: "City Treasurer's Office staff start license assessment, perform initial computation and/or apply discount, generate the Statement of Account (SOA), approve it, then confirm and approve payment"
    fee: "Mayor's Permit Fee (depends on capitalization, see Revenue Code Chapter III, Permit and Regulatory Fees Article A); Business Plate Php 200.00; Business Inspection Fee (depends on nature/type of business); Fire Safety Inspection Certificate: 15% of total fees and charges; Zoning Clearance Php 20.00; Real Property Tax Clearance Php 50.00; Sanitary Permit Php 100.00; Sanitary Inspection (based on area); Documentary Stamp Php 60.00; ICT Fee Php 25.00"
    processingTime: "1 hour and 30 minutes"
    personResponsible: "City Treasurer's Office Staff"
  - clientStep: "Claim your permits: visit the Business One-Stop-Shop (BOSS) to pick up your Mayor's Permit and Business Plate, then secure post-requirements within 30 working days (RPT Clearance, Garbage Fee, Sanitary Permit, Zoning Clearance, BFP-FSIC) to remain compliant; failure to comply is grounds for temporary closure of the business"
    agencyAction: "Assign the business plate number, view and print the Mayor's Permit, and tag it"
    fee: "None"
    processingTime: "10 minutes"
    personResponsible: "Licensing Officer II, Licensing Officer I, Administrative Assistant III"
totalProcessingTime: "2 hours and 15 minutes"
totalFees: "Php 455.00 + Mayor's Permit Fee (depends on capitalization) + 15% of total fees and charges (FSIC) + Sanitary Inspection Fee (based on area)"
---

Provides the Mayor's Permit and Business Permit for new businesses applying online through the eBOSS/eLGU system.
