---
title: "Overview"
description: "Zoning clearances, land use permits, and housing assistance offered by the city."
---

Property owners planning to build, renovate, or change the use of a property within Alaminos City need a zoning clearance from the City Planning and Development Office before applying for a building permit. The office also maintains the city's comprehensive land use plan, which guides where residential, commercial, and agricultural development is allowed.
