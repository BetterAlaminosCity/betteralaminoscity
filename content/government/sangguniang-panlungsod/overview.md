---
title: "Overview"
description: "The role of the Sangguniang Panlungsod and how to track ordinances and resolutions."
---

The Sangguniang Panlungsod is Alaminos City's legislative body, composed of the vice mayor as presiding officer and elected city councilors. It enacts local ordinances, approves the annual city budget, and confirms mayoral appointments. Ordinance and resolution records will be published in a searchable directory in a future update to this site.
